About SaVe Hospital

We Collaborate for Better Healthcare

Welcome to SaVe Hospital, a premier healthcare institution dedicated to providing exceptional medical care.

Located in the heart of Machilipatnam, our state-of-the-art facility specializes in ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat), Orthopedics, and Ophthalmology, offering comprehensive and advanced treatments tailored to your needs.

Dr. Sandeep Vemu, driven by a strong commitment to community health, envisioned and established SaVe Hospital as a comprehensive multi-speciality institution. His goal was to bring together expert care, modern medical facilities, and a patient-first approach to serve the people of Machilipatnam and beyond.”
